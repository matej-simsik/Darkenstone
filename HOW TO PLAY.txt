0. Start the game
1. New game
2. Add a new character with your name and color
3. Select your character (white border changed to purple)
4. Select opponent (these get unlocked when you progress)
5. Start game!
6. Drag & drop selected abilities (you can select the same one multiple times unless it has cooldown)
7. Finish round
8. Some abilites are cast after a round or so, these cost less mana
9. Watch your mana, health (top right) and opponent's abilities (in top window)
10. Have fun!
11. Once you win some games, you earn experience, money and stats. Check these by hovering above these in main menu
12. You may spend your money in main menu in Rewards to boost your character even more
